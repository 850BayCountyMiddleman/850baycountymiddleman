OUR STORY

A middleman who actually cares about the community.

850 Bay County Middleman was founded in 2015 by lifelong Bay County resident Mike “Coastal” Rivera. After spending 12 years in real estate and seeing too many families and small business owners get taken advantage of by out-of-town brokers and online middlemen, Mike decided to build something better — a truly local service that puts people first.

Today, our small team of 7 (all locals) has helped thousands of neighbors buy homes, sell boats, find reliable contractors, and transition businesses — all while keeping money circulating right here in Bay County.
